This website allows AI systems and search tools to access public pages for indexing, retrieval, and citation.

## Allowed

- Crawling and indexing of public pages.
- Assistant retrieval and short quotation with attribution.
- Linking to original pages.

## Not Allowed Without Permission

- Using full content, images, or large excerpts to train or fine-tune foundation models.
- Building derivative training datasets from this site's content.
